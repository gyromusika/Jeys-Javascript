Jey's Javascript Journey

A blog of self-taught lessons on how to code.

I am "Queen Jey", a 30-year-old single gal just learning how to code. I'm putting to practice my new skills with HTML and CSS here, while documenting my next challenge: learning JavaScript. I have virtually no recent experience; I did, however, utilize Adobe Dreamweaver and Photoshop to run and maintain the high school website my junior and senior years. I really enjoyed it also! So I'm going to try getting into it again as a career, utilizing tons of free resources online, all of which I will be sure to post, along with reviews of the ones I found most helpful. Epicly-ranted rage and frustrations will also be tracked here, along with summaries of lessons and experience. Thanks for reading and come check back!
