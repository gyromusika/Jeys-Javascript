Initial Beginnings

Learning the Basics, January 16th, 2018

This is my very first post as a test on my blog. I basically built the entire thing around this initial first one. I think I fixed it on mobile, if it's all fucked up on your device PLEASE LET ME KNOW. This page, as you're seeing it, was a real pain in the ass to do. Literally took me several nights to do just this, so far! I cleaned up the markup and made it less confusing- which it was, even for me; even I had a hard time reading it, and I fucking wrote it! I'm always doing little fixes and tune-ups to this page. xD Let me know if it looks funky from where you're looking at, and give me the info about your device and screen size. Screenshots are helpful also.

For all of those interested, I'm utilizing Git and Sublime Text 3 to do this. The domain name was free from freenom.com, a web hosting service based out of Amsterdam. I found the background and the quote above online through Google, but I'll try to utilize only pics and images that have been created by me, on Photoshop, from here on out. Anything otherwise, info will be noted. Thanks to my family and friends for putting up with my incessent "SEND ME SCREENSHOTS FROM YOUR DEVICE" requests!
