A Pause to Learn Responsive Design

Finding My Way: HTML & CSS, February 5th, 2018

I'm taking a break in constructing this web site to learn more about responsive displays, along with CCS frameworks and databases! I started doing this by initally utilizing the free 7-day trial over at Team Treehouse, who then charges $25/month thereafter. However, it was too easy for me to become lost in trying to follow along with the instructor. It is much too tedious to pause the video, write down some notes, try it myself, and then resume. A lot of the work was simply me figuring out what the instructor is trying to explain. I'm thankful for the free trial! xD

So after doing a bit of research on a somewhat new type of online school they call MOOC- Massive Open Online Courses, and different reviews of coding schools, I decided to go ahead and pay $19/month (utilizing the student discount. Normally, it's $29/mo) to gain access to all the courses Code School has to offer. The instructer goes into more depth helping you understand why something is the way that it is, rather than just saying, "this is how things are supposed to be, you'll learn why later". After I work on honing my markup skills, I fully plan on coming back to revamp this for mobile devices, finish the rest of the pages that are linked but not yet working, and maintain my posts as I continue to learn how to CODE! Thanks for checking back- if you mention this post, you get a FREE HUG!
